Consent to Participate in a Research Study

 

Title of Study: Dishonest Behavior in the Context of Computer Mediated Communication

 

Introduction

  • You are being asked to participate in a research study concerning user behavior on the Internet, specifically, dishonest behavior in an anonymous environment.
  • Any participant over 18 years of age may choose to participate in this study.
  • We ask that you read this form before participating in this study. At any time, you may exit the website and have your data redacted from the final findings.

 

Purpose of Study  

  • The purpose of the study is to find the likelihood of users to commit dishonest behavior on the Internet when presented with a monetary incentive.
  • Ultimately, this research will be presented as a paper to the College Board, and may be compiled into a paper created for publication in a major research publication.

 

Description of the Study Procedures

  • If you decide to participate in this study, you will be asked to do the following things: log-in with the official Google API into the website, provide your full name as proof of consent, and play a short video game.
  • The following data will be collected: IP address, Google account email address, Date of accessing the website, Study results, Full name (that you provide),

 

Risks/Discomforts of Being in this Study

  • There are no reasonable foreseeable (or expected) risks.  There may be unknown risks.

 

Benefits of Being in the Study

  • Players that complete the game before a winner is chosen will be entered into a drawing for a $25 Amazon Gift Card, with the prize given out within the month of March. Winners will be contacted via email.
  • It is also an opportunity to provide insightful data on the psychology of users on the Internet, a field often neglected in modern psychology.

 

Confidentiality

  • The records of this study will be kept strictly confidential. Research records will be kept in a locked file, and all electronic information will be coded and secured using a password protected file. We will not include any information in any report we may publish that would make it possible to identify your child. Names, email addresses, and IP addresses will be redacted from any data tables produced from the research.
  • All data is encrypted with AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) and each master key is rotated regularly on the Google servers.

 

Right to Refuse or Withdraw

  • The decision to participate in this study is entirely up to you. You may refuse to take part in the study at any time without affecting your relationship with the investigators of this study or A-TECH or losing benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.  You have the right to withdraw completely from the study at any point during the process. At any time, you may exit the website and email the address ataresearch1819@gmail.com if you would like your data to be redacted from the study results.

 

Right to Ask Questions and Report Concerns

  • You have the right to ask questions about this research study and to have those questions answered by me before, during or after the research.  If you have any further questions about the study, at any time feel free to contact me at the email address ataresearch1819@gmail.com. If you would like, a summary of the results of the study will be sent to you. If you have any other concerns about your rights as a research participant that have not been answered by the investigators, you may contact Dr. Soo Park, teacher of Advanced Placement Research class at A-TECH at 702-799-7870 or parksw1@nv.ccsd.net.
  • If you have any problems or concerns that occur as a result of your participation, you can report them to the Dr. Soo Park at the number above.

 

Consent

  • Entering a full name below indicates that you are over the age of 18, have decided to participate as a research subject for this study, and that you have read and understood the information provided above. You may receive a copy of the consent form via email within 1-5 business days of providing your data.
